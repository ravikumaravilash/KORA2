Why Medicinal Plants Matter

Medicinal plants form the foundation of Ayurveda. They represent thousands of years of observation, experience and cultural knowledge that continues to inspire modern scientific research.

Ancient Knowledge

For more than 5,000 years, Ayurveda has documented medicinal plants, their preparation, their applications, and sustainable harvesting methods.

Modern Science

Many medicinal plants are now studied worldwide to understand their bioactive compounds and potential health applications.

Conservation

Rapid urbanisation, climate change, and habitat destruction threaten many valuable medicinal plant species, making conservation increasingly important.

Preserving Ayurvedic Heritage

KORA is committed to preserving, documenting, and promoting Ayurvedic medicinal plants through digital education. By creating an interactive plant encyclopedia, future generations can continue learning about traditional knowledge while connecting it with modern scientific understanding.
